# Threedee

#### Coursework from my final year of university. Showcase for homemade 3D models made in Blender, and rendered and mounted with ThreeJS. Each model has three attached animations!

#### Models can be examined by dragging them with the cursor, and animations can be executed and switched between on a button-based carousel.
